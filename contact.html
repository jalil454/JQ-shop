— Quick Answers

FAQ

Do you restock sold-out items?

No. Every JQ piece is produced in a limited run. When a drop sells out, it is gone permanently. No restocks, ever.

How long does shipping take?

Orders ship within 3–5 business days. Delivery within Europe typically takes 2–4 days. International shipping 5–10 days.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 14 days of delivery, unworn and in original condition. Sale and limited items are final sale.

How do JQ pieces fit?

Hoodies and tees run oversized by design. We recommend going true to size. Detailed measurements are on each product page.

Are you open to collaborations?

Yes — selectively. We work with artists and creatives whose vision aligns with ours. Reach out via jalil.qesmi.2009@gmail.com.

How do I know when the next drop is?

Subscribe to our newsletter and follow on Instagram. Drop dates are announced 48 hours in advance — no exceptions.
